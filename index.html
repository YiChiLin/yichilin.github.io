I was really depressed about that. But that is not the end. I want to get closer to my dream. Therefore, I have applied for International Volunteer project in Europe. I experience a lot of cuture difference and even culture shock. It's an unforgettable period in my life. Even though I wasn't in Germany, I was still satisfied because at least I got mmore close to my dream. Once I dream, I will try my best to bring myself there.

Stay Hungry & Stay Foolish

This photo was taken during my volunteer service in Romania. There was a machine inside of the department store with which you can write whatever you want on the cans. And I took the first part of "Stay Hungry and Stay Foolish " by Steve Jobs. I keep this in my mind to inspire myself. Actually, "Stay Hungry " is an ambiguity. I teased myself with another meaning. It reflected my life in Romania. I felt hungry everywhere and everytime even though I just finished my meal thirty minutes ago.
